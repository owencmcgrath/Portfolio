VibeDeck Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 06/09/2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Built by Owen ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our VibeDeck application (the "App"). Our App is designed to provide an interface for you to view and manage your Pinterest boards and pins using the Pinterest API.

1. Information We Access via the Pinterest API

When you connect your Pinterest account to our App, we access certain information from your Pinterest profile solely as permitted by the Pinterest API and your Pinterest account settings. The information we access is limited to what is necessary to provide the core functionality of the App: allowing you to view your boards and pins and perform actions such as deleting or keeping them. This may include access to:

  • Details of your boards and pins (e.g., images, descriptions, links, board names).
  • Basic associated information about your Pinterest account (like your username), only to the extent required to display your content correctly.

We do not collect or store your Pinterest password. The App acts primarily as a secure conduit to interact with your content on Pinterest's platform.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

Our App does not directly collect or store:

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or other personal contact information.
  • Any demographic information about you.
  • User-generated content created within our App (as all content interaction is with your existing Pinterest content).
  • Information through the use of cookies, tracking pixels, or similar technologies for analytics, advertising, or other purposes.
  • Your location information (unless this is inherently part of the Pinterest API data for specific pins/boards you view, which is not directly collected by our App).

3. How We Use the Information Accessed

We use the information accessed through the Pinterest API strictly to:

  • Display your Pinterest boards and pins within the App for your viewing.
  • Enable you to manage your Pinterest content by initiating actions (like deleting or keeping pins/boards) through the App's interface, which are then processed by the Pinterest API on your Pinterest account.
  • Improve the functionality and user experience of the App specifically for these viewing and management tasks.

We do not use the information accessed for marketing, selling, or any purpose other than facilitating your interaction with your Pinterest content as described above.

4. How We Share Your Information

Given the limited information we access and the App's specific function, we do not share the information accessed from your Pinterest account with any third parties or service providers for their own purposes. We would only disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court or a government agency) or in connection with a business transfer (such as a merger or acquisition), where the information is part of the transferred assets.

5. Security of Your Information

The security of your personal information accessed through the Pinterest API is handled primarily by Pinterest's platform. Within our App, we take reasonable steps to ensure that your interaction with the Pinterest API is secure. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Your Data Protection Rights

Your primary rights regarding your Pinterest data are governed by Pinterest's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service. Since our App only accesses and interacts with your data via the Pinterest API based on your permissions, you should refer to Pinterest's policies for details on how they collect, use, and allow you to control your data.

7. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly access personally identifiable information through the Pinterest API from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used our App, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

8. Links to Other Websites

Our App may display pins or boards that contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services linked from Pinterest content.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this Privacy Policy.
